About Me

Get to know me a little better

Hey, I’m Dallin Ross! I’m a student at BYU studying Buisness Management. I love solving problems using data and numbers, and I’m especially interested in building tools that actually help people.

I’m getting married to Jena Scott on May 3rd, and I couldn’t be more excited. I come from a big family of 8 and love spending time with them. I’m also a big football guy—whether it’s watching, playing, or coaching, I’m in. I served a mission in Honduras and I’m a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, which really shapes who I am and how I live.

Outside of school, I’m all about family, faith, sports, and learning new things. Whether I’m working on a assignment, helping someone with their finances, or just playing a pickup game, I love being involved and pushing myself to grow.
