Welcome to My Football Fan Page

I love football no matter how it is involved: watching it, playing it, analyzing it, etc. I have such a passion for the sport and it has taught me so many things. I have made friends in football that I still am very close with to this day and I respect what my coaches taught me on and off the field. It is a sport that helps build you physically, mentally and emotionally. I started to play football in 5th grade and at first I was afraid of the contact, but now it is what I yearn for. I am grateful that My dad made me quit soccer and had me start to put on the pads everyday. I specialized in the position runningback and I loved to score touchdowns. The only thing that I pray for is that my kids will grow up to love football just as much as I do.
